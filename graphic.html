Designer's Note

The art of balancing colours, proportions, and typography to create visual harmony. Exploring colour theory, refining font choices, and adjusting layouts feels like crafting a rhythm that draws viewers in. Each element—shape, line, and text—comes together to communicate with clarity and appeal, making the process as rewarding as the final design itself.

Embracing an iterative design process, beginning with initial sketches and translating them into tangible prototypes using cardboard, chart paper, and XPS boards. This hands-on approach allowed for quick exploration and refinement of concepts. Utilisation of affinity mapping to synthesise insights from primary and secondary research, focusing on the needs of socially isolated individuals aged 70 and above. Through continuous feedback loops, I developed a product that facilitates intergenerational knowledge exchange, aiming to bridge generational divides and enhance social connectivity.

Conclusion

In graphic design, I focus on crafting visual solutions that are both meaningful and functional. By combining research, creativity, and design principles like color theory, typography, and balance, I strive to create designs that communicate clearly and resonate with audiences. Each project is an opportunity to merge aesthetics with purpose, ensuring impactful and user-centered outcomes.
